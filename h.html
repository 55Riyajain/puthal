Mental Health Platform Development Roadmap

Building a Wysa-like platform with AI chatbot and CBT features

Project Overview

This roadmap outlines the development of a comprehensive mental health platform featuring an AI-powered chatbot, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) tools, mood tracking, and personalized mental wellness resources.

24/7 emotional support

Evidence-based therapy
